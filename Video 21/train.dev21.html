Units in CSS

There are some units which are used in CSS.They are given below:-
  1. Pixel(px): It is 1/96 of an inch...
  2. Viewport Height(vh): This is the type of unit in which take height according to size of screen..
  3. Viewport Width(vw): This is the type of unit in which it take width according to the size of screen..
  4. em: It is the unit in which it take its size according to its parent...
  5. rem: Its is the unit in which it takes size according to its root "HTML"...
  6. vmin: It is used when any one of two is short...
  7. vmax: It is used when any one of two is big in size...
  8. %: It is used when we say that took height/width according to its parent...
