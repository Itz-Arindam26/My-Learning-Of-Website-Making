*Attributes of Video Tags*

1.width and height: Specifies the dimensions of the video.
2.poster: Specifies an image to be displayed before the video starts playing.
3.muted: Mutes the video by default.
4.loop: Repeats the video once it ends.
5.autoplay: Automatically starts playing the video when the page loads.
6.controls: Adds video controls, like play, pause, and volume.
7.src: Specifies the path to the video file.

*Attributes of Audio Tags*

1.src: Specifies the path to the audio file.
2.controls: Adds audio controls, like play, pause, and volume.
3.loop: Repeats the audio once it ends.
4.muted: Mutes the audio by default.
5.preload: Specifies if and how the audio should be loaded when the page loads ('auto', 'metadata', 'none').
