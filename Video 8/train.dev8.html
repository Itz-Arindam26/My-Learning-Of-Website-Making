About Inline elements

Inline elements do not start on a new line and only take up as much width as necessary. They are part of the flow within other elements.

Common Inline Elements are:

  • <span>
  • <a>
  • <strong>

About Block element:

Block elements start on a new line and take up whole width . They are not part of the flow within other elements.

Common Block Elements are:

  • <div>
  • <p>
  • <nav>
